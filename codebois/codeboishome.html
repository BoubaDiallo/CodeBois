About Us

We’re just kids that are scared of falling into the steady momentum that education in America breeds, just a bunch of functioning people built to do something they don’t wish to do just so society can go on and keep revolving. We’re all bred into that steady lifestyle but we want to push our ways out of it, leave our names carved into the Stonehenges of the world while we attempt to make a difference and impact the lives of others for the better. Because while we have received so much privilege ourselves to be able to think of these ideas there are others who don’t even have the basic needs to get through a normal day. People that surround us but we ignore. So we have to acknowledge this and give back to the communities we came from. Overall we’re just a bunch of kids looking to make a difference in people’s lives while furthering ourselves as professionals and people all the same.
